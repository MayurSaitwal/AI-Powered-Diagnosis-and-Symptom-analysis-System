Mission

Why this system exists

AI Medical Triage is built to help people get faster, safer direction when symptoms appear. The goal is to support early action and reduce uncertainty before clinical consultation.

AI in healthcare

Our triage flow combines structured symptom input, pattern matching, and safety-first risk scoring to produce practical next-step guidance.

What we optimize for

  • Fast initial guidance for patients
  • Conservative handling of red-flag symptoms
  • Clear, understandable language for next actions
  • Consistent triage logic across cases

Limitations

Predictions depend on the accuracy of the symptoms entered.
The system does not replace physical examination or laboratory testing.
Rare or complex co-morbid cases may require specialist evaluation.

Important Medical Notice

This application is a decision-support tool only and is not a replacement for doctors. If symptoms are severe or worsening, seek immediate professional or emergency care.
